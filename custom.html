Custom Made

A custom-created
wedding gown
for you...

Jude Jowilson, our exclusive bridal designer offers custom wedding dresses at attainable prices.

From the first sketch to the last fitting, Jude will collaborate with you to create a unique handcrafted gown that is yours alone.

Forever Amour Bridal Boutique, unlike most bridal salons, offers this couture experience.

About Jude Jowilson

Jude brings over 10 years of design experience along with a passion for understanding for brides' wants and needs. Inspired by his grandmother, a skilled home-based seamstress, he developed an interest in fashion at an early age. He continues to be inspired at Parsons The New School for Design.

Jude has joined Forever Amour Bridal Boutique as our exclusive in-house couture designer and has launched his own bridal collection under the Jude Jowilson brand.
